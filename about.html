About us

our mission
  • The term ‘Financial literacy’, is still a topic not explored by a majority of people in our country. People today do understand the importance of investing but lack the proper knowledge and guidance on how to invest smartly.
  • At pennywise, our mission is to enable fair and transparent investing through financial education to ultimately help our customers inculcate the habit of Save-Invest-Compound. We want to make investing as simple as making a cup of tea, and enable people to start with as small as ₹1, and further grow their wealth over time.

our vision
  • Our vision is to make people become financially wiser, and follow their passions instead of working for just money. We want to help people attain financial freedom so they can FIRE (Financially independent, retire early) in life, and just do what they love doing.
our core values
  • Honesty

our team
  • Our founders come with over 15 years of combined experience from diversified backgrounds of personal finance, wealth management, product development and IT services.
  • Adding to that we have a young, energetic, dedicated and highly talented team to push all limits and deliver high quality and top notch work.
  • We are mentored by some of the best industry experts who have over 50 years of combined experience and gone through the journey of making companies with rock solid foundation, time and again.
